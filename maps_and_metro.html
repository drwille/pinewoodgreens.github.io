Travel and Commuting

The entrance to the Beltway is just 1 mile away – with the Route 66 interchange just a couple of miles beyond that. And we are about 100 feet from two Metrobus lines. But more important, we’re about 2 miles away from Metro (both Dunn Loring and West Falls Church). You can walk there on snowy days. We are also only 1/2 mile from the Washington and Old Dominion (W&OD) bike trail.

We're close to all kinds of things!

We’re just 15 miles away from the nation’s capital. We’re also just 5 miles from all the stores at Tysons Corner mall, 10 miles from the music under the stars at Wolf Trap, 15 miles away from the Smithsonian, 65 miles from the unbeatable views on Skyline Drive in Shenandoah National Park , and 265 miles from the wide beaches of the Outer Banks.

Lots of improvement is coming to our area too!

Soon the Mosaic District mixed-use development will be opening on the former site of the Merrifield Multiplex adding a Target and other retail nearby. A sidewalk is being built that will connect us directly to Merrifield. Also, the Capital Beltway HOT lanes project will provide additional commuting options.
